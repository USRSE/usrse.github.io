Our **June Community Call** will be all about **When Google Fails You on Friday, June 12,** at 2pm ET/1pm CT/12pm MT/11am PT.

Have you faced a technical problem so big or obscure that not even Google was able to provide you with some answers? Was it just this one tiny change you needed to make to your config file that was nowhere to be documented, or an entire concept you missed because it wasn't actually written down anywhere in plain English? Have you experienced that sinking feeling when all Google finds for you are people complaining about your issue, but nobody shared their solution? Then this community call is for you! Come and tell us about all these weird little quirks that you have faced over the years and tell us your hero story of how you have wrestled those pesky technical details into submission! Take us along on the emotional journey as you tell us about your lowest lows and highest highs to final success!

#### Registration details

Information on how to register for the Zoom meeting will be sent via email
and posted in the `#general` channel on Slack. The registration link can also be found in the pinned messages and channel description of the `#communitycalls` channel on Slack.
