Our **March Community Call** will be all about **Legacy Code: Horrors and Successes on Thursday, March 12**, 12pm ET/11am CT/10am MT/9am PT.

Have you had the “pleasure” of resurrecting or maintaining legacy code? Do you want to share your trauma? Or maybe it was one of the easiest things you ever did in your professional career? We want to hear from you! In this month’s community call, we will talk about all the dirty little secrets you found in 5-, 10-, or even 15-year-old code and how you made it all good again! From terrifying code smells to hard-won victories, this meetup explores the horrors and successes of legacy code—and how teams survive and even thrive with it


#### Registration details

Information on how to register for the Zoom meeting will be sent via email
and posted in the `#general` channel on Slack. The registration link can also be found in the pinned messages and channel description of the `#communitycalls` channel on Slack.
