# Overview

The St. Louis Metro Regional Group connects US-RSE members in the St. Louis Metro region and organizes local events.

# Membership

The St. Louis Metro Regional Group is a <a href="{{site.baseurl}}/about/affinity-groups/">US-RSE Regional Group</a>
intended to bring together research software engineers in the St. Louis Metro region.

To stay informed, please:

* [Join US-RSE]({{site.baseurl}}/join/).
* Subscribe to event announcements by joining one of:
  * The [US-RSE Slack `#rg-stl` channel](https://usrse.slack.com/messages/rg-stl)

If you are interested in giving a talk or have other ideas for a future meetup, please contact one of the coordinators (see the [Questions?](#questions) section below).

# Activities

This affinity group aims to host a mixture of social and networking events, providing opportunities to connect with other RSEs in the St. Louis Metro region.
