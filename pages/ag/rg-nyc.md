# Overview

The New York City Regional Group connects US-RSE members in New York City and promotes Research Software Engineering in the five boroughs.

# Membership

The New York City Regional Group is a <a href="{{site.baseurl}}/about/affinity-groups/">US-RSE Regional Group</a> intended to bring together research software engineers in the five boroughs.
Members from the surrounding New York tristate area are welcome.

To stay informed, please:

* [Join US-RSE]({{site.baseurl}}/join/).
* Subscribe to event announcements by joining one of (or both):
  * The [US-RSE Slack `#rg-nyc` channel](https://usrse.slack.com/messages/rg-nyc)
  * Our [Google group](https://groups.google.com/a/us-rse.org/g/rg-nyc/about) (mailing list)

# Activities

We organize informal meetings with a focus on local in-person events within the city.
