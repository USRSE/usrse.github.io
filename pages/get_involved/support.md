volunteers to focus on advancing RSE initiatives rather than operational tasks.

## Fundraising Initiatives

Currently, US-RSE is focused on raising funds to support general operational
expenses — the modest but essential costs of membership management and member communications.
We are currently limited by the effort and time required of volunteers to maintain the current system.

### Feedback and Suggestions

We are always open to hearing comments, feedback, and suggestions from the US-RSE community on financial strategies.
Please reach out via [email](mailto:contact@us-rse.org) or the US-RSE Slack workspace.
